# Wealth Model

## Purpose

AEGIS should distinguish productive motion from compounding value.

## Wealth impact classes

- `none`
- `assisted`
- `projected`
- `realized`
- `compounding`

## What counts as wealth-positive

- a shipped monetizable asset,
- a reusable internal capability that reduces future work,
- a validated business workflow,
- or a strategic memory/skill improvement with measurable leverage.

## Required fields

- trace id,
- asset or workflow produced,
- value type,
- confidence,
- owner,
- time horizon,
- actual follow-up status.
