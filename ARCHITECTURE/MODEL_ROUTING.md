# Model Routing

## Routing classes

- **Reflex** — short low-cost interactions.
- **Design** — broader reasoning and synthesis.
- **Execute** — artifact-focused implementation.
- **Map** — consequence summarization and evidence linking.
- **None** — purely deterministic steps.

## Routing principles

- Cheapest viable model first.
- Escalate when quality is insufficient.
- Prefer local models when policy and quality allow.
- Preserve the decision path for later review.

## Quartermaster role

Quartermaster controls cost discipline and fallback decisions when premium routing is not justified.
