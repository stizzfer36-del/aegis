# Policy Model

## Purpose

Policy exists to make power safe without making the system weak.

## Triple gate

1. Pre-plan gate.
2. Pre-execution gate.
3. Post-execution verification gate.

## Always gated

- destructive actions,
- policy edits,
- secret access,
- public publishing,
- external spending,
- and wealth-affecting commitments.

## Never delegated away

The authority to bypass policy may not belong to ordinary agents.

## Required outputs

Every policy decision must return:

- decision,
- reason,
- rule matched,
- risk class,
- approval need,
- and rollback expectation.
