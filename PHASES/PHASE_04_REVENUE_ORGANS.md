# Phase 04 — Revenue Organs

## Mission

Convert AEGIS from productive to compounding.

## Ships in this phase

- Wealth impact instrumentation.
- Revenue workflow library.
- Productized artifact classes.
- Scout-assisted recon for opportunity selection.
- Loop prioritization around economic return.

## Banned in this phase

- Vanity metrics without value attribution.
- Unmapped external selling actions.

## Acceptance criteria

- AEGIS can distinguish activity from value.
- Every claimed revenue event is attributable to a traceable workflow.
- Economic decisions are logged with assumptions and outcomes.

## Proof demo

AEGIS produces, launches, and records one monetizable output with attributable trace and post-hoc review.
