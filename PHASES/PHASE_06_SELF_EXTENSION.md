# Phase 06 — Self-Extension

## Mission

Allow AEGIS to responsibly redesign parts of itself.

## Ships in this phase

- Skill promotion pipeline.
- Self-authored specs.
- Safe structural refactors.
- Controlled agent proposals.
- Regression-aware self-improvement.

## Acceptance criteria

- Self-generated changes remain phase-aligned.
- Every structural change includes rollback and proof.
- Scribe can explain why the system changed.

## Proof demo

AEGIS proposes, implements, validates, and documents a self-improvement without violating policy or clutter rules.
